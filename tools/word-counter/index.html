How to use the Word Counter

Paste your text into the box and Clickoz will instantly count words and characters. This is useful for essays, SEO drafts, social media captions, product descriptions, and email writing. The counter runs entirely in your browser, so it’s fast and privacy-friendly.

  • Words: counts separated tokens, with options to handle extra spaces and numbers.
  • Characters: total characters including spaces.
  • Characters (no spaces): helpful for SMS or strict limits.
  • Reading time: quick estimate to match platform rules.

FAQ

Does this counter work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, it runs locally in your browser with no server calls.

Why do my word counts differ from Word/Google Docs?

Different apps use slightly different rules (hyphens, apostrophes, emojis). Clickoz uses consistent, predictable parsing and gives you options.

Is my text uploaded somewhere?

No. Everything is processed on your device in real time.
