What is a word counter?

A word counter measures how many words and characters are in a text. It‚Äôs useful for SEO drafts, school assignments, blog posts, product descriptions, and any content with length constraints. Clickoz updates counts instantly as you type.

When should you use it?

  • SEO writing: match search intent, avoid thin content, and plan structure.
  • Meta descriptions & snippets: keep text clean and skimmable.
  • Editing: reduce fluff, improve clarity, and control reading time.
  • Platform limits: stay within character caps for forms and tools.

How reading time is calculated

Reading time is estimated from word count using a standard average speed (around 200 words per minute). For technical text, reading time can be higher ‚Äî use it as a quick baseline.
