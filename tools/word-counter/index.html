What is a word counter?

A word counter measures the real length of your text. It counts words and characters, including a version without spaces, and it also estimates sentences and paragraphs so you can see structure at a glance. This is useful when you write for school, work, SEO, or any platform that applies strict limits.

When should you use it?

Use a word counter when you need to check an essay word count, confirm character limits for a form, shorten a caption, or plan the structure of an article. For SEO workflows, it helps you keep paragraphs readable and estimate reading time for mobile.

How reading time is calculated

Human reading is based on a faster typical silent reading pace. AI voice reading is slower because speech is usually slower than reading. Both are estimates and are shown in seconds to stay precise.

FAQ

Does this tool upload my text?
No. The Word Counter runs in your browser, so your text stays on your device.
What counts as a word?
Words are tokens separated by whitespace. Extra spaces and blank lines are ignored.
Characters with vs without spaces?
Characters includes spaces. Characters without spaces removes whitespace before counting.
How is reading time calculated?
Human reading uses reading speed. AI voice reading uses a typical speech pace and is slower.
