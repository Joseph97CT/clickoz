What is a word counter?

      

        A word counter measures the real length of your text. It counts words and characters (with and without spaces),
        and estimates sentences and paragraphs so you can see structure at a glance.
      


      
When should you use it?

      

        Use it when you need strict limits (forms, captions, listings), or when you‚Äôre writing SEO content and want readable structure.
      


      
FAQ

      

        

          
Does this tool upload my text?

          
No. It runs locally in your browser. Your text stays on your device.

        

        

          
What counts as a word?

          
Words are tokens separated by whitespace. Extra spaces and blank lines are ignored.

        

        

          
Why show AI voice reading time?

          
Voice speaking is usually slower than silent reading, so it‚Äôs useful for scripts and narration.

        

        

          
Is reading time exact?

          
It‚Äôs an estimate. Human reading is faster than voice speaking.
