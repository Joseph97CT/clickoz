What is a word counter?

A word counter measures the real length of your text. It counts words and characters, including a version without spaces, and it also estimates sentences and paragraphs so you can see structure at a glance. This is useful when you write for school, work, SEO, or any platform that applies strict limits. Clickoz updates counts instantly while you type, so you can edit based on real numbers.

When should you use it?

Use a word counter when you need to check an essay word count, confirm character limits for a form, shorten a caption, or plan the structure of an article. For SEO workflows, it helps you keep paragraphs readable, verify that sections are balanced, and estimate reading time so content feels easy on mobile.

How reading time is calculated

Human reading time is based on a faster typical reading pace (words per minute). AI voice reading is slower because speech is usually slower than silent reading. Both are estimates and are shown in seconds so they don‚Äôt ‚Äústick‚Äù at 1 minute.

FAQ

Does this tool upload my text?
No. The Word Counter runs in your browser, so your text stays on your device.
What counts as a word?
Words are tokens separated by whitespace. Extra spaces and blank lines are ignored.
Characters with vs without spaces?
Characters includes spaces. Characters without spaces removes whitespace before counting.
How is reading time calculated?
Human reading is based on reading speed. AI voice reading is slower and uses a typical speech pace.
